Welcome to AscendFlow
Your personal CRM built for appointment setters. Track outreach, manage leads, log cash and stay on top of followups — all in one place. Let us show you around in 4 quick steps.
Tracking Sheet
Every DM you send goes here. Log the name, platform (LinkedIn, IG, X), pain point and status. Check off C, F1, F2 as you follow up — the app auto-tracks timing and warns you when 48hrs is up. Hot prospects? Hit the arrow to push them to Leads.
Leads and Cash
Qualified prospects graduate to the Leads tab where you track them through to close. When a deal lands, log it in Cash Collected — AscendFlow keeps a running total of your commissions so you always know your numbers.
One last thing
Your data is saved locally in your browser right now. Export a backup regularly from the Profile page to keep it safe. Now go log your first DM and start stacking those bookings.
